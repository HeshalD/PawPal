How Foster Care Works

1

Apply Online

Fill out our comprehensive foster application form. Tell us about your living situation, experience, and preferences.

2

Home Visit

Our team will visit your home to ensure it's safe and suitable for fostering. We'll answer any questions you have.

3

Orientation

Attend our foster orientation to learn about pet care, what to expect, and how we support you throughout the process.

4

Match & Foster

We'll match you with a pet that fits your lifestyle and experience level. Welcome your new temporary family member!

Foster Requirements

Age Requirement

Must be 21+ years old with stable housing and income to provide a safe environment.

Time Commitment

Available for daily care, feeding, exercise, and transportation to vet appointments.

Pet Experience

Previous pet experience preferred, but not required. Willingness to learn is essential.

Safe Environment

Secure home with appropriate space for the type of pet you wish to foster.

Family Agreement

All household members must agree to fostering and understand the temporary nature.

Financial Readiness

While we cover medical costs, you'll need to provide food, supplies, and emergency care.
